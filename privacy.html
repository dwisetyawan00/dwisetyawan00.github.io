Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

1. Policy Scope

This policy applies only if you deploy this application on your own infrastructure.

The seller does not collect any data from users because this is a source code product, not an online service.

2. Data Processed When You Deploy

When you deploy this application, the system will store:

  • Social media accounts you connect (access tokens, account IDs)
  • Content you schedule (titles, captions, media assets)
  • User preferences (timezone, language, team roles)
  • Activity logs (for internal audit purposes)

All data is stored in your own database (SQLite/PostgreSQL) and is not sent to external parties.

3. Encryption and Security

Social media access tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before being stored.

User sessions utilize secure cookies with HttpOnly and SameSite=Strict attributes.

You are responsible for:

  • Protecting environment variables (CRON_SECRET, DATABASE_URL, etc.)
  • Securing your deployment server
  • Implementing proper access controls and authentication
  • Regularly updating dependencies and security patches

4. Third-Party Integration

When you connect Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, or other social media accounts, data is sent directly to those platforms via OAuth authentication.

No data is transmitted to the seller or any intermediary service.

Each third-party platform has its own privacy policy and terms of service that govern how your data is handled:

  • Meta Platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Threads): Meta Privacy Policy
  • Google (YouTube): Google Privacy Policy
  • TikTok: TikTok Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

Since you control the deployment and database, you have full control over data retention policies. You can:

  • Delete data at any time directly from your database
  • Revoke social media account connections through the application interface
  • Implement custom data retention policies based on your requirements

6. User Rights

As the deployer and administrator of this application, you must ensure compliance with applicable data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) if you collect data from other users.

You are responsible for:

  • Providing appropriate privacy notices to your users
  • Obtaining necessary consents for data processing
  • Implementing data subject rights (access, deletion, portability)
  • Maintaining records of processing activities

7. Cookies and Tracking

The application uses essential cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Security features (CSRF protection)
  • User preference storage

No analytics or third-party tracking cookies are implemented by default. Any such additions are at your discretion.

8. No Seller Data Collection

The seller explicitly does not:

  • Collect, store, or process any user data from your deployment
  • Have access to your database or server infrastructure
  • Track usage analytics or telemetry from your deployment
  • Require phone-home connections or license verification that transmits data

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy applies to the version of the source code you have purchased.

Updates to this policy will be included in future releases. It is your responsibility to review the privacy policy when updating to newer versions.
